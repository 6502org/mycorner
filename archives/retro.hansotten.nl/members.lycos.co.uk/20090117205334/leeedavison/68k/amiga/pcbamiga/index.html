PCBAmiga. By Lee Davison.
Introduction

PCBAmiga is my attempt at writing a PCB and schematic CAD program for the Amiga computer. It remains unfinished due to the increasing performance and availability of commercial PC based programmes but is presented here for those who are interested.

It was developed on an '030 equipped A2000 and has been run on a stock A1200 and an '060 and Picasso IV equipped A2000 without any problems.
