Requirements. By Lee Davison.
Minimum requirements

  • 680x0 processor. A 68008 will work.
  • 16k ROM or RAM for the interpreter code.
  • 2k of RAM.
  • RS232 I/O.
    • Preferred requirements

  • 68000 or better processor.
  • 16k ROM or RAM for the interpreter code.
  • As much RAM as you're likely to need.
  • Any character based I/O (e.g. RS232, LCD/keyboard etc).
