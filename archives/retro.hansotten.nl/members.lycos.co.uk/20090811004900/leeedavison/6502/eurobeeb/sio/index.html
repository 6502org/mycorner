Euro Beeb - SIO card
CUBE - SIO. By Lee Davison.
Introduction
This card provides four extra serial ports for the Cube. The board contains the following...
  • 4 x R6551AP ACIA
  • 4 x AM26LS30PC
  • 2 x AM26LS32PC
  • R6522AP VIA
  • Misc 74 serise logic.
