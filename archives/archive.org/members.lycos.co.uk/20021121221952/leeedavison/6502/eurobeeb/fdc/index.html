Introduction
This card, as well as being the FDC, also has 4 sockets for ROM and RAM. The board contains the following...
  • TMS2793NL FDC (WD1770 software equivalent)
  • 2 x 8k RAM
  • 16k EPROM
  • 2 x PAL
  • Misc 74 series logic
