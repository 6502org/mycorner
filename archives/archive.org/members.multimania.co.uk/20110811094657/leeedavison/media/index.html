In the media

Every now and then I pop up on TV or in films. Sometimes on my own, sometimes with my dearest darling daughter. No you won't have seen us, unless you were really looking. We're extras, the human wallpaper that fills gaps and makes dramas, soaps, films or documentaries look a little more like real life.
