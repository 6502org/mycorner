Recycling stuff. By Lee Davison.
Introduction.

Sometimes things just aren't worth saving. They may be too far gone, about as rare as housebricks, or not repairable without a similar donor for parts. In this last case it's best just to keep the bits that work and bin the rest.

Examples.
  • Bulk desoldering
  • ATX power supplies
  • Ferrite core components
