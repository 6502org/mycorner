The Compukit S100 memory board.

This is an S100 memory expansion board made by Compukit, the same people who made the UK 101.

By the look of the three heatsunk 1A 5v regulators a pair of these could be used as a handy toaster as well as memory expansion.

I have a pair of these and an S100 backplane, processor, video card and floppy interface but haven't tried it all out and don't have any further information yet. So, if you have further information, or know where such can be found please e-mail me .
